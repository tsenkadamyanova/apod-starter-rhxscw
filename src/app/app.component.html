Astronomy Picture of the Day (APOD)

apod.service.ts is a predefined service that supplies data from NASA's Picture of the Day API.

Tasks

  1. Visualize the data in the apod.component.ts placeholder component.
  2. Display cards with 6 days in the past, in a layout of your choice.
  3. Add prev/next buttons to add more pages of data.
